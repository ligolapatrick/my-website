User Guides

Getting started with LoveConnect is easy. Here's a quick guide to help you:

  • Create a Profile: Fill in your details, upload a picture, and let others know about you.
  • Find Matches: Use our search and match features to find people who share your interests.
  • Update Moods etc: In features which require you to click update button, after updating leave the page or refresh the page for changes to occur.
  • Triple Click: In other features you can tap on a profile three times within 3 seconds to send a notification message.
  • Start Chatting: Send messages, like profiles, and connect with others.
  • Explore Features: Discover new people through Daily Discovery, Nearby Users, and more.
  • Join Events: Participate in virtual meetups, live streams, and speed dating sessions.
  • Check Notifications: Stay updated with notifications about matches, messages, and events.
  • Manage Privacy: Control who can see your profile and activities from the settings page.
  • Set Preferences: Adjust your notification, theme, and language preferences in the settings page.
  • Use Search: Find people based on age, location, interests, and more using the search feature.
  • Read Receipts: Manage read receipts settings to control who sees when you've read their messages.
  • Last Seen: Choose who can see your last seen status for more privacy.
  • Auto-download Media: Set preferences for auto-downloading media in chats.
  • Connect Accounts: Link your Facebook and Google accounts for easy login and sync.

If you have any questions, check out our FAQ or contact our support team.
